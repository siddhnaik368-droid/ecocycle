Construction Industry

Construction is adopting sustainable design with modular buildings, recycled concrete, and eco-friendly materials like bamboo and fly ash. Instead of demolishing old structures, materials are reused in new projects, saving energy and reducing pollution.

Electronics Industry

E-waste recycling is now vital in the electronics world. Circular strategies include device refurbishment, component recovery, and material recycling. Companies refurbish used gadgets to give them new life instead of sending them to landfills.

Agriculture Sector

In agriculture, circular practices aim for zero waste through composting, crop rotation, and renewable energy use. Farmers reuse organic residues to enrich soil and harness biogas from waste.
