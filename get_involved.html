Eco Tips for Action

For Students

  • Carry a reusable water bottle and lunchbox.
  • Start or join an eco-club at school.
  • Recycle notebooks and use both sides of paper.
  • Take part in tree-planting or campus cleanup drives.

For Citizens

  • Segregate waste into wet and dry at home.
  • Save water by fixing leaks and collecting rainwater.
  • Choose public transport or carpool to reduce emissions.
  • Support local farmers and eco-friendly brands.

For Businesses

  • Shift to paperless offices and digital records.
  • Recycle packaging and use renewable materials.
  • Offer incentives for customers who reuse containers.
  • Power offices with solar or other green energy sources.
